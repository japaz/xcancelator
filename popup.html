Monitoring X.com and Twitter.com URLs

How it works:

  • Redirects X.com and Twitter.com URLs to xcancel.com
  • Preserves the path, query parameters, and hash
  • Skips redirect for root domains (x.com, twitter.com)
  • Skips redirect when navigating within the same domain
